## 🔴 Finding 01 — Delivery Delay is the #1 Driver of Customer Dissatisfaction

**Priority:** `CRITICAL`

### 🔍 Finding
Delayed orders average **2.56 stars**, compared to **4.26 stars** for on-time deliveries — a **1.70-star gap**, making delivery speed the strongest determinant of customer satisfaction.

Post-holiday periods (Feb–Mar 2018) show recurring backlog effects, where operational overload leads to delayed fulfillment and a surge in negative reviews.

### ⚡ Impact
- ~10% of customers fall into high churn risk due to poor delivery experience
- 1-star reviews significantly reduce platform trust and brand perception
- Lack of delivery visibility increases customer frustration during delays

### ✅ Action
- Implement **real-time delivery tracking alerts** to improve transparency
- Introduce a **delayed-order recovery workflow** (apology + voucher + re-rating request)
- Enforce **SLA KPIs with logistics partners** to ensure consistent delivery performance

## 🔴 Finding 02 — Northeast Region Has Systemic Logistics Failures

**Priority:** `CRITICAL`

### 🔍 Finding
Low-performing states (AL, MA, SE) consistently show below-average ratings, confirming a strong correlation between logistics performance and customer satisfaction.

| State | Avg. Rating |
|---|---|
| AL | 3.83 |
| MA | 3.79 |
| SE | 3.89 |
| Platform Avg | ~4.13 |

### ⚡ Impact
- Persistent underperformance risks long-term customer loss in these regions
- Weak logistics performance limits regional growth potential
- Negative experiences may spread via word-of-mouth and reviews

### ✅ Action
- Audit logistics partners in underperforming states
- Explore regional fulfillment or last-mile delivery partnerships
- Implement **region-specific SLA targets and monitoring**

## 🟠 Finding 03 — Weekend Demand Gap Represents Recoverable Revenue

**Priority:** `HIGH`

### 🔍 Finding
Weekend order volume is consistently lower than weekdays, averaging **134 vs 174 orders/day (23% gap)** across Oct 2016 – Aug 2018.

| Day | Orders |
|---|---|
| Mon | 9.1K |
| Tue | 8.9K |
| Wed | 8.8K |
| Thu | 8.3K |
| Fri | 7.3K |
| Sat | 6.6K |
| Sun | 5.9K |

### ⚡ Impact
- Revenue growth can be achieved without acquiring new customers
- Indicates underutilized traffic during weekend browsing periods

### ✅ Action
- Launch **weekend-exclusive promotions**
- Send **Friday evening push/email campaigns**
- Run **A/B tests on weekend discount strategies**

## 🔴 Finding 04 — Security & Services Category Shows Critical Customer Satisfaction Weakness

**Priority:** `HIGH`

### 🔍 Finding
The **Security & Services** category has the lowest satisfaction level with an average rating of **2.50 stars**, significantly below other categories (~3.62–3.77 stars).

Additionally, **20% of reviews are 1-star**, indicating severe and recurring dissatisfaction patterns.

### ⚡ Impact
- High churn risk due to extremely poor customer experience
- Damages overall platform trust and credibility
- Increases customer support workload and complaints

### ✅ Action
- Conduct root cause analysis (service quality, sellers, fulfillment issues)
- Remove or improve low-performing sellers/products
- Strengthen after-sales support and communication processes

## 🟢 Finding 05 — Revenue is Concentrated in Core Product Categories

**Priority:** `MEDIUM`

### 🔍 Finding
Top revenue contributors (Jan 2017 – Aug 2018):

- Health & Beauty (R$1.46M)
- Bed Bath & Table (R$1.44M)
- Watches & Gifts (R$1.31M)
- Sports & Leisure (R$1.16M)
- Computer Accessories (R$1.12M)

### ⚡ Impact
- Strong dependency on a small number of categories
- High opportunity for cross-selling and upselling strategies

### ✅ Action
- Prioritize marketing for top-performing categories
- Introduce bundling and recommendation systems
- Strengthen supply chain and seller partnerships

---

## 🟢 Finding 06 — São Paulo (SP) Dominates Platform Revenue

**Priority:** `HIGH`

### 🔍 Finding
São Paulo (SP) contributes **65.03% of total revenue**, making it the dominant geographic driver of platform performance.

### ⚡ Impact
- High dependency risk on a single state
- Any disruption in SP significantly impacts total revenue
- Indicates underdeveloped opportunities in other regions

### ✅ Action
- Strengthen retention strategies in SP
- Expand marketing and seller acquisition in underperforming states
- Optimize logistics to maintain SP service quality
- Diversify revenue across multiple regions
